# Reflection Types

Reflection operates in specific structural forms.

---

## 1. State Reflection
Examines:
- current global state  
- active transitions  
- structural load  

---

## 2. Boundary Reflection
Examines:
- layer role separation  
- functional drift  
- cross-layer contamination  

---

## 3. Transition Reflection
Examines:
- transition paths  
- threshold validity  
- failed transitions  

---

## 4. Stability Reflection
Examines:
- coherence  
- collapse potential  
- resonance pressure  

---

## 5. Schema Reflection
Examines:
- semantic alignment  
- naming consistency  
- identifier correctness
