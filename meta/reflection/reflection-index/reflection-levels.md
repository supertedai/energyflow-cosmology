# Reflection Levels

Reflection can occur at different levels of structural altitude.

---

## Level 1 — Local Reflection
Examines specific boundaries and local behaviour.

---

## Level 2 — Intermediate Reflection
Examines interactions across adjacent layers.

---

## Level 3 — Global Reflection
Examines:
- global dynamics  
- stability fields  
- load maps  
- transition relations  

---

## Level 4 — Meta Reflection
Examines:
- architecture-wide patterns  
- systemic drift  
- recursion behaviour  
- meta-process alignment
