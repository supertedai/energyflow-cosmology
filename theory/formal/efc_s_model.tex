\section{EFC-S: Entropy Field}

The structural sector (EFC-S) defines an entropy-like field
$S(\bm{x})$ which encodes the degree of information content, 
organization or anisotropy in the system.

\subsection{Entropy Gradient}

The entropy gradient is defined as:
\begin{equation}
    \bm{g}_S(\bm{x}) = \nabla S(\bm{x}).
\end{equation}

In the implementation:
\begin{equation}
    \bm{g}_S = k_S \,\nabla S.
\end{equation}

This gradient interacts directly with the energy-flow potential 
and the expansion mapping.
