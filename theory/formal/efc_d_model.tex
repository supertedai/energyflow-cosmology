\section{EFC-D: Energy-Flow Dynamics}

The dynamical sector governs the relationship between the entropy 
field and the energy-flow field $E_f(\bm{x})$.

\subsection{Energy-Flow Potential}

We define an effective potential:
\begin{equation}
    \Phi = A_\Phi \, F(E_f, S),
\end{equation}
where $F$ is an interaction term specified in the implementation.

A minimal working model used in the code is:
\begin{equation}
    \Phi(E_f, S) = A_\Phi \, E_f \,(1 + S).
\end{equation}

\subsection{Coupling to Structure}

The potential determines circular velocity and local curvature via:
\begin{equation}
    v^2(r) \propto r \frac{\partial \Phi}{\partial r}.
\end{equation}
