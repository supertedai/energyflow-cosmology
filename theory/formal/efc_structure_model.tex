\section{EFC-Structure: Halo Formation and Φ-Equilibria}

Structure formation in EFC is governed by equilibrium conditions
in the energy-flow potential.

\subsection{Structural Equation}

Stable configurations satisfy the condition:
\begin{equation}
    \nabla \Phi(E_f,S) = 0.
\end{equation}

This defines EFC halos.

\subsection{Entropy–Flow Coupling}

Including the entropy gradient gives:
\begin{equation}
    \nabla \Phi + S \nabla S = 0.
\end{equation}

This yields profiles of the form:
\begin{equation}
    \Phi(r) = A_\Phi E_f(r)(1 + S(r)).
\end{equation}

These profiles predict rotation curves without dark matter and
large-scale distribution without a cosmological constant.
