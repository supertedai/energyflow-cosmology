Abstract

Energy-Flow Cosmology (EFC) treats the universe as a thermodynamic information system driven by gradients in energy flow and entropy.

The theory is organised into three tightly coupled base layers:

  • EFC-S: structural and halo-level descriptions,
  • EFC-D: energy-flow dynamics,
  • EFC-C₀: entropy–information mapping.
