Abstract

Energy-Flow Cosmology (EFC) treats the universe as a thermodynamic information system driven by gradients in energy flow and entropy. Instead of introducing invisible matter or energy components, EFC starts from energy distribution, entropy gradients and information capacity.

  • EFC-S: structural and halo-level descriptions
  • EFC-D: energy-flow dynamics
  • EFC-C₀: entropy–information base layer

1. Frontmatter

This document is the canonical master specification for Energy-Flow Cosmology (EFC). It defines the relations between EFC-S, EFC-D and EFC-C₀ and provides a compact formal baseline.

2. Overview

A central object is the local energy-flow potential:

$$E_f(\mathbf{x}) = \rho(\mathbf{x})\,(1 - S(\mathbf{x}))$$

4. Part I — EFC-S: Structure / Halo Layer

S₀. Low-entropy anchors

EFC-S starts from low-entropy anchors: regions where $\langle s \rangle \ll$ background. These act as seeds for structure.

S₁. Halo Model of Entropy

Halos are described by $(\rho_h(r), S_h(r))$, not by density alone.

S₂. Radial profiles

$$\rho_h(r) = \rho_0\,f(r/r_s), \qquad S_h(r) = S_0 + (1-S_0)\,g(r/r_c).$$

5. Part II — EFC-D: Energy-Flow Dynamics

D₀. Local energy-flow potential

$$E_f = \rho\,(1-S).$$

D₁. Mass density

$$\rho = \frac{m}{V}.$$

D₂. Flow rate

$$\frac{dE_f}{dt} = (1-S)\frac{d\rho}{dt} - \rho\frac{dS}{dt}.$$

D₃. Spatial gradients

$$\nabla E_f = (1-S)\nabla\rho - \rho\nabla S.$$

D₄. Effective acceleration

$$\mathbf{a} \propto -\nabla E_f.$$

D₅. Expansion

$$H = H_0\,F(E_f,S).$$

6. Part III — EFC-C₀

C₀. Information capacity

$$I(\mathbf{x}) \propto \rho(\mathbf{x})\,(1-S(\mathbf{x})).$$

C₁. Cognitive load

$$L = I_{\text{used}}/I_{\text{tot}}.$$

C₂. Informational coupling

$$\frac{dI}{dt} = \alpha\,\frac{dE_f}{dt} - \beta\,D_I.$$

7. Appendix: Symbols

Symbol Meaning Notes
$\rho$ Mass density $\rho = m/V$
$S$ Dimensionless entropy Scaled to $[0,1]$
$E_f$ Energy-flow potential $E_f = \rho(1-S)$
$\nabla_t$ Time derivative Along chosen time variable
$\rho_h(r)$ Halo density profile EFC-S
$S_h(r)$ Halo entropy profile EFC-S
$I(\mathbf{x})$ Information capacity EFC-C₀
$L$ Local cognitive load $L = I_{\text{used}}/I_{\text{tot}}$
$H$ Expansion rate Derived, not primary
$H_0$ Reference expansion scale To be calibrated
$\alpha,\beta$ Couplings Information–flow links
