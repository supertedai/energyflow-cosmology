## Abstract
This paper explores how **energy flow** functions as the underlying scaffolding of spacetime — the thermodynamic substrate that maintains curvature, coherence, and continuity across the universe.  
Rather than treating spacetime as an empty geometric manifold, it proposes that **energy flow and entropy dynamics** continuously sustain its fabric.  
Through both theoretical reasoning and observational analogies, it links gravitational curvature, entropy increase, and quantum energy fluctuations into one unified continuum.

---

## 1. Introduction
Traditional physics defines spacetime curvature as a consequence of matter-energy density per Einstein’s field equations.  
However, this study reframes that relation: **spacetime is not merely curved by energy — it is sustained by its flow**.  

Energy flow \(E_f\) acts as a **self-regulating thermodynamic field**, maintaining equilibrium between high-energy (low-entropy) and low-energy (high-entropy) regions.  
This introduces a continuous dynamic between gravity, expansion, and entropy production.

---

## 2. Core Principles

### 2.1 Energy Flow as a Universal Constant
At cosmological scales, total energy flow remains constant, while locally it fluctuates with entropy gradients.  
\[
E_f(S) = E_0 (1 - S)
\]
where \(S\) represents normalized entropy.  
Thus, local structures (galaxies, clusters) maintain curvature by adjusting energy flow relative to entropy change.

---

### 2.2 The Fabric of Space-Time
Spacetime is modeled as a **thermodynamic lattice** rather than a static geometry.  
Each point exchanges energy with its surroundings, governed by the divergence relation:
\[
\nabla \cdot [k(S) \nabla E_f] = \frac{\partial S}{\partial V}(E_f, S)
\]
Curvature becomes an emergent property of the energy-flow potential.

---

### 2.3 Entropy as a Driver of Geometry
Entropy increases drive continuous adjustments in local curvature:
- In low-entropy domains, energy flow converges (gravitational attraction).  
- In high-entropy domains, energy flow diverges (expansion).  
This duality replaces dark energy and dark matter with measurable **entropic flux**.

---

### 2.4 Local vs. Cosmic Energy Balances
Energy flow can be **anisotropic** — coherent locally (e.g., galaxies) but dissipative cosmically (e.g., voids).  
This creates hierarchical stability: small-scale order within large-scale entropy increase, consistent with observed cosmic structure.

## 4. Hypothesis Development
Energy flow acts as the **scaffold of spacetime**, dynamically adjusting to maintain geometric and thermodynamic balance.  
The universe is therefore:
- **Self-regulating**, not externally fine-tuned.  
- **Continuous**, not created or destroyed.  
- **Thermodynamically coherent**, maintaining constant total energy through entropy-driven redistribution.

## 7. Conclusion
Spacetime exists as an **active thermodynamic medium**, maintained by continuous energy flow between entropy extremes.  
Curvature, gravity, and expansion emerge as different expressions of this flow, unified under a single principle:
> **Entropy directs energy; energy sustains spacetime.**  

This hypothesis extends **General Relativity** into the thermodynamic domain, forming one of the foundational elements of the **Energy-Flow Cosmology (EFC)** framework.
