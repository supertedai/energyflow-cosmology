# Energy-Flow Cosmology v1.2  \
**Foundational Framework and Cross-Field Continuity**\
\
This preprint formalizes the covariant, non-equilibrium thermodynamic field framework extending General Relativity.\
It introduces the scalar energy-flow potential Ef as the generator of entropy-driven curvature and structure,\
provides proofs of mathematical well-posedness and Lyapunov stability, and links the framework to\
informational and biological domains through the free-energy correspondence.\
