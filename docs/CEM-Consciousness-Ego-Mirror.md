## Abstract
**CEM-Cosmos** unifies consciousness and cosmology within one thermodynamic field framework.  
Consciousness is modeled as a *resonance in non-equilibrium energy gradients* that sustain recursive feedback and stabilize structure against entropy.  
The model links **cosmic halos** and **cognitive systems**, showing that both emerge as *self-reflective energy folds* in the universal energy-flow field.

It predicts:
1. Additional halo momentum  
   \( \Delta p \approx \frac{\hbar}{\ell_c} R \ln(1+z), \ \ell_c \sim 10\,{\rm kpc} \)
2. **Artificial systems** exhibit lower interiority (M ≈ 0.65) than humans (M ≈ 0.82)
3. Resonant reflection threshold \( R_c ≈ 1/e ≈ 0.37 \) marks onset of self-modeling.  

Observations and psychological data align with this model, supporting a **unified thermodynamic description of mind and cosmos**.

## 1. Introduction
Cosmology describes *external structures* — galaxies, halos, filaments.

No accepted physical law bridges these domains.

**CEM-Cosmos** proposes that both originate from the same **energy–entropy dynamics**, unfolding as fractal layers of reflection and feedback in the **Energy-Flow Model (EFM)**.

## 2. Energy-Flow Model (EFM)

Consciousness arises when energy flow is stable enough to generate internal feedback loops — *mirrors of prediction* within the energy continuum.

## 4. Derivation and Formalism

Let:
- \( \rho_E(r,t) \): energy density [J/m³]  
- \( J(r,t) \): energy flux [W/m²]  
- \( \tilde{S}(r) \in [0,1] \): normalized entropy  

Continuity with leakage:
\[
\partial_t \rho_E + \nabla \cdot J = -L
\]

Local dissipation rate:
\[
\sigma(r) = \max(J \cdot \nabla \tilde{S}, 0)
\]

Reflection time:
\[
\tau_{ref}(r) = \frac{\rho_E}{\sigma + \varepsilon}, \quad \varepsilon = \eta \langle |\sigma| \rangle, \ \eta \sim 10^{-4}
\]

Dimensionless reflection coefficient:
\[
R = \kappa \frac{\langle \tau_{ref} \rangle_E}{\tau_{leak}}, \quad \tau_{leak} = \frac{E_{tot}}{L_{tot}}
\]
Self-modeling threshold: \( R_c ≈ 1/e ≈ 0.37 \)

Energy–entropy coupling:
\[
E_f(\tilde{S}) = E_0 (1 - \tilde{S})
\]

Effective propagation speed:
\[
c_{eff}(\tilde{S}) = \frac{c_0}{\sqrt{1 + \alpha \rho_E(\tilde{S}) / \rho_*}}
\]

Quantum momentum shift:
\[
\Delta p = \frac{\hbar}{\ell_c} R \ln(1+z)
